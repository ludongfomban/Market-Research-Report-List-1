The market for these valves can be categorized into two main types: straight-through and angled designs. </p><p>Straight-through choke relief valves facilitate a direct flow path, minimizing pressure drops and ensuring efficient operation. In contrast, angled choke relief valves feature a 90-degree change in direction, which can reduce space requirements and optimize installation in tight environments. Each type offers distinct advantages based on application needs.</p></p>

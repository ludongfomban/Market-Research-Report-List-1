<p><p>The commercial cash register market encompasses various types, primarily categorized into single-screen and double-screen systems. Single-screen registers feature one display for both the cashier and customers, offering a straightforward and cost-effective solution for retail environments. In contrast, double-screen registers include separate displays for the cashier and customers, enhancing transparency and interaction during transactions.

<p><p>Heat shrink tunnel machines for plastics utilize different heating methods to shrink plastic films around products. Convective (hot-air) type uses hot air to evenly distribute heat, ensuring consistent shrinkage. Radiant (infrared) type employs infrared radiation, directly targeting the film for quick heating and efficient shrinkage. Steam type utilizes steam to heat the shrink film, providing a moist environment that facilitates uniform shrinkage without overheating the product. Each type offers unique advantages based on product requirements and production speed.</p></p>

<p><p>The electric scrubber dryer market is segmented into two main types: walk-behind scrubber dryers and stand-on/ride-on scrubber dryers. Walk-behind scrubber dryers are compact and maneuverable, ideal for smaller spaces and tight areas, allowing operators to stand behind the machine while cleaning. In contrast, stand-on or ride-on scrubber dryers are designed for larger areas, offering increased efficiency and operator comfort as they allow users to stand on or ride while cleaning, thus covering bigger surfaces in less time.</p></p>

<p><ul><li>Welding Type</li><li>Thread Type</li><li>Flange Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Intelligent Pneumatic Butterfly Valve market features three main types: Welding Type, Thread Type, and Flange Type. Welding Type valves are permanently fixed using welding, ensuring a tight seal and ideal for high-pressure applications. Thread Type valves rely on threaded connections, allowing for easy installation and disassembly, making them suitable for moderate pressure systems. Flange Type valves use flanges for bolted connections, providing flexibility for maintenance and alignment in larger piping systems. Each type caters to specific operational needs and installation environments.</p></p>

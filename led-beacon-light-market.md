<p><strong>The LED Beacon Light Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>High Intensity Lights (HIL)</li><li>Medium Intensity Lights (MIL)</li><li>Low Intensity Lights (LIL)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The LED Beacon Light market is categorized into three types based on intensity: High Intensity Lights (HIL), Medium Intensity Lights (MIL), and Low Intensity Lights (LIL). HILs are designed for high visibility in challenging environments, often used in airports and tall structures. MILs offer a balance of visibility and energy efficiency, suitable for medium-risk areas like communication towers. LILs provide a more subdued illumination for low-risk applications, typically used in rural areas and smaller infrastructures, prioritizing safety without overwhelming brightness.</p></p>

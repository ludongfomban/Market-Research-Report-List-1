<p><p>Soft contact lenses are thin, flexible lenses made from hydrophilic (water-attracting) materials that conform to the shape of the eye. They provide vision correction for refractive errors such as myopia, hyperopia, and astigmatism, and are favored for their comfort and ease of use compared to traditional rigid lenses. The market for soft contact lenses has been witnessing substantial growth due to factors such as an increase in the prevalence of vision disorders, a growing aging population, and rising awareness regarding eye health.</p><p>Technological advancements have led to the development of innovative products, including daily disposable lenses and lenses designed to correct presbyopia. Additionally, the rise of e-commerce has made purchasing soft contact lenses more convenient, further fueling market expansion. The Soft Contact Lens Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.3% during the forecast period, driven by these trends and the increasing adoption of contact lenses over spectacles among consumers. Overall, the soft contact lens market is poised for continued expansion in the coming years.</p></p>

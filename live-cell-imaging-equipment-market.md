<p><p>The live cell imaging equipment market includes various advanced microscopy techniques, notably Confocal Laser Scanning Microscopes (CLSM) and Spinning-Disk Confocal Microscopes (SDCM). CLSM uses focused laser beams to create high-resolution, 3D images of specimens, enhancing visualization of cellular structures in live cells. In contrast, SDCM employs multiple overlapping laser beams to achieve faster imaging and reduced phototoxicity, making it ideal for capturing dynamic cellular processes. Both technologies are essential for research in cell biology, developmental biology, and drug discovery.</p></p>

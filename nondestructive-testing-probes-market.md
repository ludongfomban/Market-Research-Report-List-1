<p><p>The Nondestructive Testing Probes market encompasses various sensor types, including Split Type Eddy Current Displacement Sensors and Integrated Eddy Current Displacement Sensors. Split Type Eddy Current Sensors offer flexibility as they separate the sensor from the electronics, making them suitable for hard-to-reach areas. In contrast, Integrated Eddy Current Sensors combine the sensor and electronics into a single unit, enabling easier installation and reduced wiring complexity. Both types are essential for precise displacement measurement in industrial applications, contributing to quality control and equipment maintenance.</p></p>

Chromatography instruments, including HPLC and GC, separate chemical compounds for accurate analysis, ensuring reliable results in complex mixtures. Breathalyzers measure alcohol content in breath samples, providing immediate results for compliance and safety. Collectively, these instruments enhance drug screening efficiency and accuracy in clinical and forensic settings.</p></p>

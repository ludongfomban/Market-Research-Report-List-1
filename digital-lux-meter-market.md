<p><strong>The Digital LUX Meter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Visual LUX Meter</li><li>Photoelectric LUX Meter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The digital LUX meter market is primarily divided into two main types: visual LUX meters and photoelectric LUX meters. Visual LUX meters are designed to approximate human visual perception, measuring illuminance in terms of how light affects the human eye. In contrast, photoelectric LUX meters utilize photodiodes or phototransistors to measure light intensity more accurately, capturing a broader spectrum of light conditions. Both types are essential for applications in photography, horticulture, and workplace lighting assessments, enhancing energy efficiency and safety.</p></p>

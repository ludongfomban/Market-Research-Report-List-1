<p><ul><li>In-Band</li><li>Guard Band</li><li>Standalone</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Narrow Band IoT (NB-IoT) markets can be categorized into three types: In-Band, Guard Band, and Standalone. </p><p>In-Band uses existing LTE spectrum, enabling efficient integration with current cellular networks. Guard Band occupies unused frequency slots between LTE channels, providing an effective buffer to minimize interference. Standalone operates on dedicated frequency bands, allowing for optimized performance and reduced reliance on other networks. Each type addresses specific deployment needs, enhancing connectivity for IoT devices in various applications, from smart metering to urban infrastructure.</p></p>

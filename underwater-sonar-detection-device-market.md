<p><p>The underwater sonar detection device market is primarily segmented into multi-channel and single channel types. Multi-channel sonar systems utilize multiple transmitting and receiving channels, enabling enhanced detection capabilities and greater accuracy in complex underwater environments. This makes them suitable for applications requiring detailed mapping and real-time data processing. In contrast, single channel sonar devices operate with one channel, providing simpler and often more cost-effective solutions for basic detection tasks. Both types serve diverse needs in marine exploration, security, and research.</p></p>

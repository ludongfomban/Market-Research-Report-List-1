<p><ul><li>Isolated</li><li>Non-isolated</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The CAN FD transceiver market is divided into two main types: isolated and non-isolated transceivers. Isolated transceivers provide electrical isolation between the CAN bus and the device, enhancing protection against voltage spikes and grounding issues, making them suitable for harsh environments. Non-isolated transceivers, on the other hand, offer simpler designs with lower costs and are widely used in applications where electrical isolation is not critical. Both types cater to specific needs in automotive and industrial communication systems.</p></p>

<p><p>Cardiac assist devices are critical tools in supporting heart function for patients with severe heart conditions. Ventricular Assist Devices (VADs) are mechanical pumps that augment blood flow from the ventricles, often used in advanced heart failure cases. Intra-aortic Balloon Pumps (IABPs) are temporary devices that improve coronary blood flow and reduce cardiac workload by inflating and deflating a balloon in the aorta. Both devices play essential roles in managing heart failure and supporting patients awaiting transplant or other therapies.</p></p>

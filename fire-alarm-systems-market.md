<p><strong>The Fire Alarm Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Addressable Systems</li><li>Conventional</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fire alarm systems are primarily categorized into two types: addressable systems and conventional systems. Addressable systems use advanced technology to identify the exact location of a fire, allowing for quicker response times and precise information during emergencies. Each device has a unique address, enabling centralized monitoring. On the other hand, conventional systems group detectors into zones, triggering alarms without pinpointing specific locations. While conventional systems are generally simpler and more cost-effective, addressable systems offer enhanced functionalities and scalability for larger facilities.</p></p>

<p><p>The UV additives market consists of three main types: heat stabilizers, light stabilizers, and UV absorbers. Heat stabilizers protect polymers from thermal degradation during processing and application, enhancing durability under heat exposure. Light stabilizers prevent deterioration from ultraviolet light, extending the lifespan of materials by reducing color fading and structural damage. UV absorbers absorb harmful UV radiation and transform it into heat, preventing polymer breakdown. Together, these additives enhance the longevity and performance of various products exposed to environmental stressors.</p></p>

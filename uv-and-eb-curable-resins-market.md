<p><strong>The UV and EB Curable Resins Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Radical Curable Resin</li><li>Cationic Curable Resin</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The UV and EB curable resins market includes radical curable resins and cationic curable resins. Radical curable resins utilize ultraviolet light or electron beams to initiate polymerization, forming durable coatings and adhesives. They are commonly used in applications like inks and coatings. Cationic curable resins, on the other hand, rely on cationic polymerization triggered by UV light, allowing for enhanced thermal and chemical resistance. Both types are preferred for their quick curing times and low volatile organic compound emissions, supporting eco-friendly practices.</p></p>

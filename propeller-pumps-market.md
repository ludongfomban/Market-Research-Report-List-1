<p><p>The propeller pumps market is categorized into vertical and horizontal propeller pumps, each serving distinct applications. Vertical propeller pumps, designed for deep-water lifting, feature a vertical shaft that allows efficient movement of fluids in confined spaces. They are commonly used in irrigation, drainage, and industrial processes. In contrast, horizontal propeller pumps are suited for surface-level installations, facilitating easier maintenance and operation. Their design enables effective fluid transfer in applications like water supply and wastewater treatment, enhancing performance across various sectors.</p></p>

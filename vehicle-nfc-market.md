<p><ul><li>106 Kbit/S Vehicle NFC</li><li>212 Kbit/S Vehicle NFC</li><li>424 Kbit/S Vehicle NFC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Vehicle NFC market is categorized into three types based on data transfer speeds: 106 Kbit/s, 212 Kbit/s, and 424 Kbit/s. The 106 Kbit/s variant is primarily used for basic applications, such as vehicle access and simple payments. The 212 Kbit/s version offers enhanced speed for features like vehicle diagnostics and user identification. The 424 Kbit/s type provides the fastest data transfer, enabling complex applications such as interactive infotainment systems and real-time vehicle monitoring, enhancing user experience and functionality.</p></p>

<p><p>The 3D motion capture suits market can be categorized into body-based and image-based types. Body-based motion capture systems utilize wearable suits embedded with sensors that track movements in real-time, providing precise data for applications like animation and biomechanics. In contrast, image-based systems rely on cameras to capture motion from the environment without the need for special suits, using algorithms to analyze visual data. Both types serve various industries, including entertainment, sports, and healthcare, enhancing the accuracy and efficiency of motion analysis.</p></p>

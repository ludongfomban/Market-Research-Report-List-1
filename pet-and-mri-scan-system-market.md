<p><strong>The PET and MRI Scan System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Traditional PET-MRI Systems</li><li>Helium-free PET-MRI Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PET and MRI scan system market comprises two primary types: traditional PET-MRI systems and helium-free PET-MRI systems. Traditional PET-MRI systems utilize helium-cooled superconducting magnets, offering high-resolution imaging but facing challenges related to helium supply sustainability and cost. In contrast, helium-free PET-MRI systems employ innovative technology that eliminates the need for helium, making them more environmentally friendly and cost-effective, while still providing high-quality imaging capabilities. The shift towards helium-free systems reflects a growing emphasis on sustainability in medical imaging.</p></p>

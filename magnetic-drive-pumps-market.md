<p><strong>The Magnetic Drive Pumps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Stationary Shaft</li><li>Rotating Shaft</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Magnetic drive pumps are designed to transfer fluids without direct contact between the motor and the pump, enhancing efficiency and safety. The market can be divided into two main types: stationary shaft and rotating shaft. Stationary shaft magnetic drive pumps maintain a fixed motor while the magnetic coupling moves the impeller, ideal for applications requiring stability. In contrast, rotating shaft pumps possess a rotating motor that drives the impeller directly, offering flexibility for varying fluid viscosities and flow rates. Both types effectively minimize leakage and maintenance needs.</p></p>

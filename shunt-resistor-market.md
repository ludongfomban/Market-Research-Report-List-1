<p><ul><li>SMD Resistor</li><li>Through Hole Resistor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The shunt resistor market is divided into two main types: SMD (Surface-Mount Device) resistors and through-hole resistors. SMD resistors are compact, designed for mounting directly onto the surface of printed circuit boards, allowing for automated assembly and higher packing density. In contrast, through-hole resistors are inserted into drilled holes in the PCB, providing mechanical stability and ease of replacement. Both types serve essential roles in measuring current and voltage drop in various electronic applications, catering to different design requirements.</p></p>

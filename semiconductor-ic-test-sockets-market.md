<p><p>The semiconductor IC test sockets market encompasses various types, including BGA (Ball Grid Array), QFN (Quad Flat No-lead), and WLCSP (Wafer Level Chip Scale Package), along with others. BGA sockets accommodate solder balls for compact connections, while QFN sockets support flat package configurations without leads, promoting efficient thermal performance. WLCSP sockets facilitate testing directly at the wafer level, reducing production costs. Each type serves specific applications, enhancing testing accuracy and efficiency, crucial for the semiconductor industry's reliability and performance standards.</p></p>

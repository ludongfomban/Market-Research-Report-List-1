<p><p>The torque rheometer market is primarily divided into two types: single screw rheometers and twin screw rheometers. Single screw rheometers are designed for simple processing applications, providing reliable viscosity measurements and representing a cost-effective solution. In contrast, twin screw rheometers offer enhanced mixing capabilities and better temperature control, making them suitable for complex formulations and higher viscosity materials. Both types serve critical roles in polymer processing and material characterization, catering to diverse industry needs.</p></p>

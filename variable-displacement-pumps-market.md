<p><ul><li>Variable Displacement Piston Pump</li><li>Variable Displacement Vane Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The variable displacement pumps market comprises primarily two types: variable displacement piston pumps and variable displacement vane pumps. Variable displacement piston pumps utilize pistons to adjust flow based on pressure changes, offering high efficiency and precise control, making them ideal for heavy-duty applications. In contrast, variable displacement vane pumps use rotating vanes to modulate flow, providing smoother operation and lower noise levels, suitable for lighter applications. Both types cater to diverse industries, including construction, agriculture, and manufacturing.</p></p>

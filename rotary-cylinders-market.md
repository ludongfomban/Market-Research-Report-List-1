<p><p>The rotary cylinders market is segmented into three main types: pneumatic, hydraulic, and electric rotary cylinders. Pneumatic rotary cylinders operate using compressed air, making them suitable for applications requiring quick, precise motion. Hydraulic rotary cylinders rely on fluid pressure, offering high torque capabilities ideal for heavy-duty tasks. Electric rotary cylinders utilize electrical energy for operation, allowing for precise control and programmability, making them popular in automation and robotics. Each type caters to specific industrial needs based on force, speed, and precision.</p></p>

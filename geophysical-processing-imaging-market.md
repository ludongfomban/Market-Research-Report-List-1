<p><p>The Geophysical Processing & Imaging Market encompasses various techniques used for subsurface exploration. Ground Penetrating Radar (GPR) employs radar pulses to image the underground, ideal for locating utilities or archaeological features. Electromagnetic methods utilize electromagnetic fields to detect soil and rock properties, useful in mineral exploration. LIDAR (Light Detection and Ranging) measures distances using laser reflections, creating high-resolution topographical maps. Other technologies in this market include gravity and seismic methods, each serving distinctive applications in environmental, geological, and construction sectors.</p></p>

<p><p>Small Business CRM software is available in two main deployment types: on-premises and cloud-based. On-premises CRM involves installing software directly on local servers and computers, providing businesses with complete control over data and security, albeit with higher upfront costs and maintenance responsibilities. In contrast, cloud-based CRM is hosted on remote servers, accessible via the internet, offering flexibility, scalability, and lower initial investment. This model allows for easier updates and collaboration, making it popular among small businesses seeking cost-effective solutions.</p></p>

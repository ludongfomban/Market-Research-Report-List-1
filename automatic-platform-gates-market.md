<p><strong>The Automatic Platform Gates Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Full Height Closed Automatic Platform Gates</li><li>Half Height Open Automatic Platform Gates</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automatic Platform Gates (APG) market primarily consists of two types: Full Height Closed APGs and Half Height Open APGs. Full Height Closed APGs are designed to completely enclose the platform area, providing enhanced safety and minimizing accidental falls or access to the tracks. In contrast, Half Height Open APGs offer partial barriers, allowing better visibility and airflow while still enhancing passenger safety. Both types cater to different transit systems and passenger needs, influencing their adoption across various rail networks.</p></p>

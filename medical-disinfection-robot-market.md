<p><p>The medical disinfection robot market features two primary types: UV-C disinfection robots and HPV (Hydrogen Peroxide Vapor) disinfection robots. UV-C disinfection robots utilize ultraviolet light to eliminate pathogens on surfaces and in the air, offering rapid and chemical-free sanitization. HPV disinfection robots, on the other hand, disperses hydrogen peroxide vapor to achieve thorough disinfection in various healthcare settings, effectively reaching challenging areas. Both types enhance infection control and improve safety in medical environments, catering to the growing demand for advanced disinfection solutions.</p></p>

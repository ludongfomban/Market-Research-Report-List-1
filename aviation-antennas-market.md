<p><strong>The Aviation Antennas Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Directional Antenna</li><li>Omnidirectional Antenna</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aviation antennas market is primarily segmented into two types: directional and omnidirectional antennas. Directional antennas are designed to focus radio waves in a specific direction, enhancing communication range and signal strength for targeted applications such as air traffic control and point-to-point links. In contrast, omnidirectional antennas transmit and receive signals in all directions, providing broader coverage and reliability, making them ideal for applications requiring constant connectivity, such as in-flight communication systems and passenger services.</p></p>

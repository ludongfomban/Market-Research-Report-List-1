<p><ul><li>Eddy Current Dynamometer</li><li>AC Dynamometer</li><li>Hydraulic Dynamometer</li><li>High Speed Dynamometer</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Engine dynamometers measure engine performance and efficiency across various types. Eddy Current Dynamometers utilize electromagnetic forces to create a load, ideal for test applications requiring precise control. AC Dynamometers use alternating current for power absorption, suitable for diverse engine testing. Hydraulic Dynamometers employ fluid resistance to provide consistent load conditions, effective for heavy-duty testing. High Speed Dynamometers are designed for fast-revving engines, ensuring accurate measurements at elevated speeds. Other types may include specialized dynamometers for niche applications, offering tailored performance analysis.</p></p>

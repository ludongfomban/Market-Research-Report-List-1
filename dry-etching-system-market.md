<p><ul><li>The Conductor Etching</li><li>Dielectric Etch</li><li>Polycrystalline Silicon Etching</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dry etching system market encompasses three primary types: conductor etching, dielectric etching, and polycrystalline silicon etching. Conductor etching focuses on patterning metals and conductive layers, essential for semiconductor devices. Dielectric etching targets insulating materials, critical for creating isolating layers between components. Polycrystalline silicon etching is specialized for etching silicon structures in solar cells and microelectronics. Each type employs various gas chemistries and techniques, making them vital for the precision manufacturing processes in the electronics industry.</p></p>

<p><p>The Ultrasonic Doppler Diagnostic Equipment market primarily includes two types: Ultrasound Doppler Vascular Imaging and Color Doppler Flow Imaging. Ultrasound Doppler Vascular Imaging focuses on assessing blood flow in vessels and identifying circulatory disorders by utilizing sound waves to measure the velocity of blood flow. Color Doppler Flow Imaging enhances this by visually representing blood flow direction and velocity through color coding, enabling more accurate diagnostics of vascular conditions and improving treatment planning in clinical settings.</p></p>

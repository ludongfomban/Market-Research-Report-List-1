<p><strong>The Industrial Resin 3D Printer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>LFS Technology</li><li>FDM Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Industrial Resin 3D Printer market is primarily divided into two key technologies: LFS (Lithography-Based Fast Sintering) and FDM (Fused Deposition Modeling). LFS Technology utilizes a light source to cure liquid resin, enabling the production of high-resolution and complex geometries, while achieving faster build times. Conversely, FDM Technology involves extruding thermoplastic filaments layer by layer, offering versatility and cost-effectiveness, making it suitable for a broad range of applications. Both technologies cater to different industrial needs and preferences.</p></p>

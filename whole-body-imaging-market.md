<p><p>The Whole-Body Imaging market includes advanced imaging techniques such as Computed Tomography (CT) scans and Positron Emission Tomography (PET) scans. CT scans use X-rays to create detailed cross-sectional images of the body, aiding in the diagnosis of various conditions. In contrast, PET scans utilize radioactive tracers to visualize metabolic processes, enabling the identification of tumors and assessing organ function. Both imaging modalities are essential in oncology, cardiology, and neurology, enhancing disease detection and treatment planning.</p></p>

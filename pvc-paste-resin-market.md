<p><ul><li>Micro Suspension Method</li><li>Emulsion Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PVC Paste Resin market is primarily categorized into two production methods: the Micro Suspension Method and the Emulsion Method. The Micro Suspension Method involves dispersing fine PVC particles in a liquid medium, resulting in a highly stable and uniform resin suitable for applications like sealants and coatings. In contrast, the Emulsion Method combines PVC with water-based emulsifiers, producing a more flexible resin ideal for diverse applications, including adhesives, textiles, and automotive parts. Both methods offer unique advantages for end-use industries.</p></p>

<p><ul><li>Single Acting Type</li><li>Double Acting Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electro-pneumatic positioners market primarily features two types: single acting and double acting. Single acting positioners utilize compressed air to move the actuator in one direction, relying on a spring for the return motion, making them simpler and cost-effective for certain applications. In contrast, double acting positioners provide more precise control by using air pressure for both directions of movement, offering greater responsiveness and accuracy in automation processes. This differentiation caters to various industrial requirements and complexities.</p></p>

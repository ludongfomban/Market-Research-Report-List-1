<p><p>Needle-free drug injection systems are categorized into various types based on their mechanism of delivery. Jet-based needle-free injectors use high-velocity jets of liquid to penetrate the skin, ideal for quick vaccinations. Spring-based injectors use mechanical springs to propel medication, offering a reliable and efficient method. Laser-powered injectors utilize focused laser energy to create microchannels in the skin for drug delivery. Vibration-based injectors employ high-frequency vibrations to facilitate transdermal drug absorption, enhancing comfort and efficiency in administration.</p></p>

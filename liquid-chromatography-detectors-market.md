<p><p>The liquid chromatography detectors market includes various types designed to analyze compounds. UV-Visible detectors measure absorbance at specific wavelengths, ideal for colored or UV-active substances. Refractive index detectors gauge changes in the refractive index of the mobile phase, suitable for non-UV-active compounds. ELSD detectors, or evaporative light scattering detectors, detect non-volatile compounds by measuring light scattering. Fluorescence detectors offer high sensitivity by detecting fluorescence emissions. Other detectors encompass mass spectrometry and conductivity detectors, expanding analytical capabilities in diverse applications.</p></p>

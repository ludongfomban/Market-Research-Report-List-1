<p><p>Adenosine Deaminase Deficiency (ADA-SCID) drugs primarily include Adagen and Revcovi. Adagen, the first approved enzyme replacement therapy, is aimed at replenishing the deficient enzyme in patients, promoting immune function and reducing infections. Revcovi, a newer treatment, offers a pegylated form of the enzyme, which enhances its stability and duration of action, allowing for less frequent dosing. Both drugs address the same underlying deficiency but cater to different patient needs and treatment preferences within the ADA deficiency market.</p></p>

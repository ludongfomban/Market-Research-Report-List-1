<p><p>Stretch wrapping equipment is categorized into fully automatic and semi-automatic systems. Fully automatic stretch wrappers operate independently, wrapping products without manual intervention, ideal for high-volume operations, enhancing efficiency and consistency. In contrast, semi-automatic stretch wrappers require some manual setup, allowing for flexibility in wrapping varying product sizes. They are suitable for medium-volume operations, providing a balance between automation and operator involvement. Both types are pivotal in ensuring load stability and protection during storage and transportation.</p></p>

<p><p>The optical microscope market is categorized into three main types: monocular, binocular, and trinocular microscopes. Monocular microscopes feature a single eyepiece, providing a straightforward viewing experience, ideal for basic applications. Binocular microscopes have two eyepieces, offering enhanced depth perception and comfort, making them suitable for extended use and detailed examinations. Trinocular microscopes include a third eyepiece for camera attachment, facilitating documentation and imaging, catering to research and educational environments where high-quality visuals are essential.</p></p>
